My name is Navie Narula and I am currently a graduate student at Columbia University studying computer science and digital journalism.

I am fascinated by natural language processing. In particular, I hope to explore the potential applications it could have in uncovering literary messages in a tangible way for individuals. Information retrieval in terms of text mining and sentiment analysis in regards to social media sites have also sparked my interest. I am an advocate for storytelling via probabilistic methods and data visualizations—but also via paper.

Ideally, I would like to work as a software engineer and have artificial intelligence or human-computer interaction play a role in my daily work. I want to write code impactfully. Beyond my coursework, I imagine to do so in a software engineering internship this upcoming summer (2017).

I also love connecting with others and learning more about other people. Please feel free to reach out to me if you would like to collaborate on a project, especially a literary or technical one!
