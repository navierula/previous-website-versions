In academia, I have been published by both Boston University and Harvard University. At Boston University, my essay regarding epiphanies in James Joyce's Dubliners won an appearance at the institution's annual WR Journal. Similarly, at Harvard University, my piece on the neglection of Mrs. Dalloway's Septimus Warren-Smith was selected for publication among other summer scholars at the college.
