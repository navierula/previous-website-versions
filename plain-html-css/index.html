My name is Navraj (Navie) Narula and I am a graduate student at Columbia University studying computer science with a special focus on natural langauge processing and computational journalism. I completed my undergraduate degree at Boston University, where I also studied computer science along with English literature and secondary education.

I am passionate about examining large datasets for interesting stories and coming up with algorithms to perfect textual analysis. I am also interested in machine learning as it pertains to pattern recognition and user behavior.
